## What is callMechanic?

**callMechanic** is a free, GPS-based platform that instantly connects vehicle owners with skilled mechanics nearby — whether you drive a hatchback, a heavy-duty truck, a JCB on a worksite, or even own a boat or generator.

Post a problem, see who's available within kilometres of you, compare ratings & prices, and pick the mechanic you trust. **No middleman. No commissions. No markups.**

## 👥 Who's it for?

=== "🚗 Vehicle Owners"
    Stuck on the road, due for a service, or just need a quick check-up?
    Post your problem in **30 seconds**, see nearby mechanics within minutes,
    and pick the one with the best ratings & price for you.

=== "🔧 Mechanics"
    Tired of waiting for walk-ins? Get **direct, paid leads** from owners
    near your shop. List your services, working hours, and specializations.
    Doorstep work optional.
