## 📋 What gets deleted

Within **7 days** of your request, the following data is **permanently removed** from our servers:

| Data | Deleted |
|---|---|
| Your **account** (name, phone, password hash, role) | ✅ Yes |
| Your **profile photo** and any **shop photos** *(mechanics)* | ✅ Yes |
| Your **GPS location** *(shop or job)* | ✅ Yes |
| Your **shop details** — name, address, hours, services *(mechanics)* | ✅ Yes |
| Your **vehicle expertise**, specializations, facilities | ✅ Yes |
| Any **job postings you created** *(owners)* and their photos | ✅ Yes |
| Any **feedback** you submitted | ✅ Yes |
| Your **socket session** and live location pings | ✅ Yes (immediate) |

Once deletion is complete, the data is **non-recoverable**.

## 📂 What may be retained

A small amount of data may be kept for legal or operational reasons:

| Data | Retention | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Anonymised job records** (with your identity removed) | Indefinite | Allows historical ratings and reviews of the *other party* in a completed job to remain intact |
| **Anonymised ratings you gave or received** | Indefinite | Other users' ratings depend on these — we keep them with your identity stripped |
| **Server access logs** (IP + timestamp of requests) | 7 days | Standard security log rotation; auto-deleted |
| **Backup snapshots** that still contain your data | Up to 30 days | Database backups are encrypted and rotate out automatically |

After **30 days**, no trace of your data remains anywhere in our system, including backups.

## ❓ Frequently asked

### Can I delete just my photos but keep my account?

Yes — email us asking for that specifically. We'll delete the specified photos and confirm.

### Can I reactivate after deletion?

No. After deletion you can register a fresh account using the same phone number, but the previous data (jobs, ratings, photos) cannot be restored.

### What about jobs I'm currently part of?

If you have a job that's **`accepted`** or **`in_progress`**, please complete or cancel it before requesting deletion. We won't delete data tied to an active job, because the other party still needs to access it.

### Can I just stop using the app?

Yes — if you don't open the app, no new data is collected. But your account remains in our database until you formally request deletion. We don't auto-delete inactive accounts.

### Will I get a confirmation?

Yes. We will reply to your email confirming:
1. The deletion request was received
2. The deletion has been completed (within 7 days)
3. A final note when backup snapshots containing your data have rotated out (within 30 days)
